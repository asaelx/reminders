Reminders for a better life

I believe that the meaning of life is the life itself, to live it, to enjoy it. Don't dwell too much on the past, learn from it, appreciate the good things, have a plan for your life but don't worry too much about the future, focus on today and give your best everyday, develop good habits, be kind, be grateful, learn something new everyday and your future will be inevitably amazing. Live today to the fullest because "now" is all we really have after all.

— Asael Jaimes.
